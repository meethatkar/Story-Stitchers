Welcome to Story Stitchers!

How to Play

  1. Click the Start button to begin.
  2. Select your level thoughtfully; each level offers unique challenges.
  3. Select your category as per your needs.
  4. Read the provided story hint carefully.
  5. Choose the most appropriate words to complete the story.
  6. Click Submit to view your generated story.

Rules

  1. Read each story prompt carefully.
  2. Ensure your responses are relevant to the ongoing story.
